stb_truetype.h is required for this library.

You can download it from: https://github.com/nothings/stb

To download it:
1. wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nothings/stb/master/stb_truetype.h
   OR
2. curl -O https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nothings/stb/master/stb_truetype.h

Place stb_truetype.h in this directory.
